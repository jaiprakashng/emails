Sathsang - For Isha meditators only
 

Sathsang means "Communion with Truth". The Sathsang creates an environment in which we can become more receptive to higher states of awareness. It is an opportunity for us to come together, focus and recharge ourselves in the company of other meditators.

Please note that this sathsang is open only to those members who have completed online Inner Engineering programme or both IE/Shambhavi Mahamudra in one of the centres.

To offer the taste of this monthly group meditation to all of us, one of the Ishangas will be flying from Melbourne, Australia. We request you to contribute to share and offset this expense after the session.

Date: 16th July 2016, 4pm - 6:15pm

Venue: Shanti Niwas Seniors Centre, 14 Spring Street, Onehunga, Next to the Dolphin Theatre, Auckland

Contact No: 02102763956 / 0221099771

  • This is also a unique opportunity for those who want to correct or restart Shambhavi Mahamudra kriya.

Bhava Spandana - Oct 08- 11, 2016
 

Bhava Spandana Program (BSP) is a 4-day, 3-night, residential program offered to those who have been initiated into Shambhavi Mahamudra. This advanced meditation program is designed by Sadhguru to provide an opportunity to experience higher levels of consciousness, beyond the limitations of body and mind. BSP offers the experience of a world of limitless love and joy.

The word Bhava literally means “sensation.” Spandana can be loosely translated as “resonance.” Through powerful processes and meditations, the Bhava Spandana Program creates an intensely energetic situation, where individuality and the limitations of the five sense organs can be transcended, creating an experience of oneness and resonance with the rest of existence.

For more details, visit ishayoga.org.au/bhava-spandana

Venue: PGL Campaspe Downs, 1302 Trentham Road, Kyneton, VIC 3444
