 {{> drop-shadow }} {{> drop-shadow }} Bhava Spandana - Oct 08- 11, 2016 {{> spacer-white}}

Bhava Spandana Program (BSP) is a 4-day, 3-night, residential program offered to those who have been initiated into Shambhavi Mahamudra. This advanced meditation program is designed by Sadhguru to provide an opportunity to experience higher levels of consciousness, beyond the limitations of body and mind. BSP offers the experience of a world of limitless love and joy.

The word Bhava literally means “sensation.” Spandana can be loosely translated as “resonance.” Through powerful processes and meditations, the Bhava Spandana Program creates an intensely energetic situation, where individuality and the limitations of the five sense organs can be transcended, creating an experience of oneness and resonance with the rest of existence.

For more details, visit ishayoga.org.au/bhava-spandana

What is Life Without Intoxication?

Question: Namaskaram Sadhguru, Since BSP, my hatha yoga practices are so intense. After every posture, I feel intoxication and even when I do Shambhavi, I feel the same. What is happening?

Sadhguru: Tell me, what is life without intoxication? What is life without awareness? The only reason you know you are alive is that you are somewhat aware. The basis of your existence is awareness. If you kill your awareness, you have taken away life itself. If you are dry and aware, it is still good because you could see things, but if you can be totally intoxicated and still be aware, isn’t it wonderful? That is what Bhava Spandana is – to get you intoxicated but fully conscious, no hangover. If you want, you can switch it off; if you want, you can switch it on. This is nice and it doesn’t cost anything. You can be on endlessly – for twenty-four hours you can be drunk, and for twenty-four hours you can be fully conscious.
