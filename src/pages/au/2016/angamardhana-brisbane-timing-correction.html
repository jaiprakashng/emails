We look forward to welcoming you to the Angamardhana program on Saturday 20 August at the St Augustine's Anglican church, Hamilton. Due to an internal error on our part the session timings have not been listed correctly.

The session timings are

  • 8:30 am to 12:00 pm
  • 5:00 pm to 8:30 pm

The reason for the second session to start at 5PM is to ensure that you have had a gap or at least four hours after lunch before you begin the second session. Having said this, our Hatha Yoga teacher will make announcements at the venue and will do his best to end the second session earlier. We deeply regret if this is going to cause anyone inconvenience.

Venue and parking: The venue is easily accessible on racecourse road. There is a small car park that can also be used on a first come first served basis. Off street parking is also available. The street is lined with several restaurants as well.

Other information: Please bring your yoga mat, a water bottle. Wear loose, comfortable clothes. Though there are restaurants opposite to the venue it is recommended that you bring a wholesome lunch for this program.

You can stay in and around the venue in the afternoon. We will offer two free session in the afternoon. These are Isha Upa Yoga (2 pm - 3 pm) and Isha Kriya guided meditation (3:30 pm - 4:30 pm). You are welcome to join these sessions.

We look forward to seeing you on Saturday morning.

Please Note:
  • It is mandatory to attend all the sessions
  • You need to be on empty stomach, when you come for the program
  • Kindly arrive atleast 15 mins before the program start time, so that you are settled before the program starts
  • Hall doors will close sharply at the program start time and latecomers will not be allowed inside
