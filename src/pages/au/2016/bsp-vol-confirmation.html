PROGRAM INFORMATION

Date : Oct 7, 2016☆; to Oct 11, 2016
Venue : PGL Campaspe Downs Country Resort, 1302 Trentham Road, Kyneton, Victoria, Australia

WHAT TO BRING

Weather can fluctuate during the month of October. Be prepared for wet weather and cold nights. Please pack a jacket and extra shoes along with the following items:

  • If you are driving to the venue, please bring your own sleeping bag and/or blankets. (Pillow and Fitted Sheet will be provided with Mattress)
  • Yoga mat - if needed for your practices
  • Flashlight
  • Toiletries & towel (shampoo, soap, toothpaste etc.)
  • Hiking shoes
  • Comfortable and warm clothing, enough for 5 days
  • Rain jacket or umbrella
  • Insect repellent

Click here for local weather updates.

* Appropriate Attire: For your own comfort and to support the program, kindly avoid wearing tight-fitting or revealing clothing. Simple, loose-fitting clothes would be most comfortable.
