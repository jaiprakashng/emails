 {{> drop-shadow }} Monthly Group Meditation (Sathsang) Isha Monthly Group Meditation is an opportunity to reconnect with your kriya with Sadhguru's voice guiding you through the Shambhavi Mahamudra Kriya. Here are the details of our upcoming Sathsang:

Date & Time: 13 August, 5:00 PM to 7:15 PM
Venue: Kings Park Villa Club House, 1 Kings Park Road, Yau Ma Tei ( Map)

Inner engineering online participants, who are not initiated into shambhavi, can join after Shambhavi practice is over. Please be outside the door by 6.35 pm. We will open the doors as soon as Shambhavi practice is over to let you in and once again close the door to avoid disturbance to others.

 {{> drop-shadow }} {{> drop-shadow }} Shambhavi Mahamudra Program In order to make sure that the participants have enough time to finish Inner Engineering Online, it is highly recommended for everyone to register before 15 August. Please spread the word!

For registration and more information, please visit ishafoundation.org/hk/smm.

We also invite those who have been initiated into Shambhavi Mahamudra to volunteer for this program. This is a great opportunity to go through this again and deepen your experience. If you wish to be a part of this, please do write back to us. {{> drop-shadow }} Bhava Spandana Program Bhava Spandana Program (BSP) is a 4-day, 3-night, residential program offered to those who have been initiated into Shambhavi Mahamudra. This advanced meditation program is designed by Sadhguru to provide an opportunity to experience higher levels of consciousness, beyond the limitations of body and mind. BSP offers the experience of a world of limitless love and joy.

For more details, visit ishayoga.sg/bhava-spandana
