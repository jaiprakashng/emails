 {{> drop-shadow }} Monthly Group Meditation (Sathsang) {{> spacer-white-small}} We welcome you to the next Isha Monthly Group Meditation. This is an opportunity for you to reconnect and correct your practice of the Shambhavi Mahamudra Kriya by doing the Kriya guided by Sadhguru's voice. Here are the details of our upcoming Sathsang:

Date & Time: 10 September, 9:00 AM to 11:15 AM
Venue: Viva Dance, 9th Floor, Dawning House, 145 Connaught Road Central, Sheung Wan ( Map)

Inner engineering online participants, who are not initiated into shambhavi, can join after Shambhavi practice is over. Please be outside the door by 9.35 AM. We will open the doors as soon as Shambhavi practice is over to let you in and once again close the door to avoid disturbance to others.

 {{> drop-shadow }} {{> drop-shadow }} Bhava Spandana Program {{> spacer-white-small}}

Bhava Spandana Program (BSP) is a 4-day, 3-night residential program offered to those who have been initiated into Shambhavi Mahamudra. This advanced meditation program is designed by Sadhguru to provide the opportunity to go beyond the limitations of body and mind and experience higher levels of consciousness. BSP offers the experience of a world of unbounded love and joy.

* Limited seats left

Gents Program: Sep 10th to 13th
Ladies Program: Sep 15th to 18th

Venue: MBA Long Hua, Kulai, Johor, Malaysia
