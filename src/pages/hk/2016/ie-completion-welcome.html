(2 mins walk from Sheung Wan MTR Station Exit C) {{> drop-shadow }} Bhava Spandana Program {{> spacer-white-small }}

Bhava Spandana Program (BSP) is a 4-day, 3-night residential program offered to those who have been initiated into Shambhavi Mahamudra. This advanced meditation program is designed by Sadhguru to provide the opportunity to go beyond the limitations of body and mind and experience higher levels of consciousness. BSP offers the experience of a world of unbounded love and joy.

Gents Program: Sep 10th to 13th
Ladies Program: Sep 15th to 18th

Venue: MBA Long Hua, Kulai, Johor, Malaysia

{{> drop-shadow }} Isha Kriya Programs {{> spacer-white-small }}

Isha Kriya is a powerful 12-minute meditation process designed by Sadhguru for anyone seeking to instill a drop of spirituality in their lives. It is free, simple, and easy to practice. Available as a guided meditation with an instructional video and downloadable instructions, it has the potential to transform the life of anyone who is willing to invest just a few minutes a day.
