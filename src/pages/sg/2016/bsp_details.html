We wanted to remind you that the early bird offer (available for limited seats) for Bhava Spandana program, offered in September in Johor, Malaysia will be ending soon (latest by 23rd August).

Please click below to hear experiences of few participants, who attended Bhava Spandana at same location last year.

Below are few highlights of this program:

  • Chinese translation will be offered for first time for Bhava Spandana. Limited seats are available.
  • 12th September is a public holiday. So for gents program, only one day leave on 13th is needed to attend the program.
  • We are also providing bus transportation to and from the secure premise of the program venue in Kulai, Johor, Malaysia at a nominal cost from Singapore. Volunteers will be there to take the participants to the venue and bring them back. If interested, please choose this while registering.
  • The venue is a Buddhist meditation centre (below) and is located in serene, peaceful and secure premises.

Sadhguru has often spoken about how Bhava Spandana can be an important turning point in one’s spiritual journey and many years of regular sadhana can be covered in just these three and half days of this programme.

That’s why, we offer this programme in various cities besides Isha Yoga Centre. No matter where it is conducted, through Sadhguru’s grace, an intensely energetic situation is created, where individuality and the limitations of the five sense organs can be transcended, creating an experience of oneness and resonance with the rest of existence.
