We are excited to announce that a Bhava Spandana Program (BSP) will be offered in Johor, Malaysia in September 2016, jointly organised by Isha Malaysia and Isha Singapore.

Bhava Spandana Program (BSP) is a 4-day, 3-night residential program offered to those who have been initiated into Shambhavi Mahamudra. This advanced meditation program is designed by Sadhguru to provide the opportunity to go beyond the limitations of body and mind and experience higher levels of consciousness.

The Bhava Spandana program, no matter where it is conducted creates an intensely energetic situation, where individuality and the limitations of the five sense organs can be transcended, creating an experience of oneness and resonance with the rest of existence. Sadhguru has now made this program available in many localities besides the Isha Yoga Center, as this is an important step in ones Inner journey and he wants as many people to take this pivotal step as soon as possible.

{{> spacer-white-small }}

"I carried a mountain of pain and fear with me into BSP and I thought that this was a burden I would bear for the rest of my life. By the end of the program, I laid that burden down for good and walked away from it. I felt that I was truly free to start anew and chose how I wanted to live my life. The indescribable love, joy and connection to life that I experienced in BSP still resides within me and sustains me today."

- Shamala, BSP Participant, Malaysia

 {{> spacer-white-small }}

The early bird offer for the Bhava Spandana program, will end on 23 August. In order to make the best use of this program, one needs to prepare a certain way in advance. So, we strongly encourage those who wish to participate to register early and make use of this offer while it's still available. The better you prepare, the better the experience of this program could be for you.

We are happy to announce that Chinese translation will also be provided for limited seats.
