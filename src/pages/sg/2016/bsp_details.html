We wanted to remind you that the early bird offer (available for limited seats) for Bhava Spandana program, offered in September in Johor, Malaysia will be ending soon (latest by 23rd August).

Please watch below videos to hear the experiences of few participants, who attended Bhava Spandana at same location last year.

Below are few highlights of this program:

  • We are really pleased to announce that for the first time ever, we are offering chinese translation for Bhava Spandana for limited seats.
  • Please note 12th September is a public holiday. So for gents program, you need to take leave just for one day on 13th in order to attend this program.
  • We are also providing bus transportation to and from the secure premise of the program venue in Kulai, Johor, Malaysia at a nominal cost from Singapore. Volunteers will be there to take the participants to the venue and bring them back. If you are interested in availing this transport, choose this while registering.
  • The venue itself is located in serene, peaceful and secure premises. Please find below few pictures of the last Bhava Spandana program conducted at the same venue.

At various times, Sadhguru has stressed about how Bhava Spandana is an important turning point in one’s spiritual journey and many years of regular sadhana can be covered in just these three and half days of Bhava Spandana. That’s why, Sadhguru has encouraged us to make this program available in various cities besides Isha Yoga Centre. No matter where it is conducted, through Sadhguru’s grace, an intensely energetic situation is created, where individuality and the limitations of the five sense organs can be transcended, creating an experience of oneness and resonance with the rest of existence.
