"My aim in this book is to help make joy your constant companion. To make that happen, this book offers you not a sermon, but a science; not a teaching, but a technology; not a precept, but a path" - Sadhguru

This new year, there is a book that can take one on a journey of self transformation. It is not a one-time read, but a systematic, scientific guide that you can continuously refer to and apply daily to become an architect of a joyful life.

The latest book by Sadhguru, Inner Engineering: A Yogi's Guide To Joy, is an invitation to go beyond words.


Sadhguru describes the book as "a mix of perspective and practice, of explanation and experiment, of insight and the chance to test every hypothesis in the laboratory of life. This makes it unique." The book contains 24 simple practices and self-observation exercises that one can immediately apply in their life based on what is most appealing.
