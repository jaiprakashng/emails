Namaskaram,


The full moon day in the month of Ashadha (July-August) is known as Guru Purnima. This sacred day honors the ancient lineage of enlightened beings who have graced the world with their presence.

And a Guru is like a road-map; without the guidance of such a teacher one may eventually reach the desired destination, but it could take a very long time.

The day of Guru Purnima is traditionally the time when seekers express their gratitude to the Guru and receive his blessings. Guru Purnima is also considered an especially beneficial day to practice our yoga and meditation.

Join us for an evening of meditation, joy, and Grace...
Date: Tuesday, 19th July
Time: 6pm to 8pm
Venue: Sri Ruthra Kaliamman Temple,
100 Depot Rd, Singapore 109670

If you wish you may bring prasadam (sweets and flowers) as an offering to the Guru.

If you are unable to come but still would like to celebrate this at home, you can follow the instructions below. It would be good to light a lamp and some incense before hand, and sit in a quiet, undisturbed place.

Instructions to celebrate Guru Purnima at home on the evening of July 19th:

  • If you have Sadhguru Sannidhi at home:
    • Follow the Sannidhi Process
  • Otherwise:
    • Sit together at 6:30pm
    • Silence (10 minutes)
    • Aum chanting for 10-12 minutes
    • Brahmananda Swaroopa Chant (20 minutes)
    • Silence (5 minutes)
