Isha Yoga Program

7 Day Program

 August 2016 {{> drop-shadow }}

Inner Engineering with the Shambhavi Mahamudra Kriya
by a trained teacher


We are happy to announce that, after 5 years Isha Singapore is re-introducing the live Inner Engineering program designed by Sadhguru and conducted by a trained teacher.

Inner Engineering is a comprehensive system, derived from the ancient science of yoga, to establish a deep and lasting personal transformation. It is a method of aligning your body, mind, emotions and energies to function at their peak and in complete harmony.

The program includes Shambhavi Mahamudra Kriya - a powerful 21-minute inner energy process that incorporates the breath; when practiced regularly, this 21-minute kriya can bring the following various benefits into one's life.

{{> drop-shadow }} Volunteering {{> spacer-white }}

Many of you have expressed a desire in the past to go through Inner Engineering Online program again. After 5 years, this opportunity has become available again for all of us to go through Live Inner Engineering program. This is an ideal opportunity for us to listen to Sadhguru again as a volunteer and imbibe the tools of Inner Engineering to a much deeper and experiential level.
