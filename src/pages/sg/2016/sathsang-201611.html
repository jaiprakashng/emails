Isha Monthly Group Meditation is an opportunity to reconnect with your kriya with Sadhguru's voice guiding you through the Shambhavi Mahamudra Kriya. Here are the details of our upcoming Sathsang:

Date & Time: 5 November, 6:00 PM to 8:15 PM
Venue: SCCCI Building, Auditorium, Level 2, 47 Hill Street #09-00, (5 mins walk from City Hall MRT Station Exit B)
Singapore 179365 (Map)

Please ensure that

  • you are on empty stomach by 6pm
  • you are seated in the hall at least 15 minutes before the start time (by 5.45 pm) as late comers will not be permitted.

Inner Engineering Online and Mystic Eye participants, who are not initiated into Shambhavi, can join after Shambhavi practice is over. Please be outside the door by 6.35 pm. We will open the door approximately around this time as soon as Shambhavi practice is over to let you in and once again close the door to avoid disturbance to others.

* Please note that Sathsang will be conducted on the 2nd Saturday of every month across all Asia Pacific centers including Singapore starting from Jan 2017.
