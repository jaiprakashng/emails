Do you remember your first encounter with Sadhguru?

Whether it was that video you accidentally clicked that ended up captivating you, or that book you happened to pull off a bookstore shelf and could not stop reading, or that television channel you flipped… you found him! His wisdom, piercing logic and deep level of clarity, lit a spark within. And, since that moment, our lives have been so distinctly enriched that in various ways we have asked ourselves, "How do I share this?"

Some choose to speak up and inspire others. Some cannot put their experience into words. Some become involved as volunteers and teachers. Some, with fully loaded careers and family lives, find their own ways, from small to big, as undercover yogis, to sneak in a little yoga into their lives and into that of those around them.

If you are looking for ways to share this, please read on and inspire others to experience Sadhguru this January and, in turn, help light up a spark within others.
